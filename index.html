FAQs

What should I wear?

We're having a semi-formal celebration. Think cocktail attire - dress to impress but keep it comfortable for dancing!

Will there be vegetarian/dietary options?

Yes! We'll have options for various dietary requirements. Please let us know about any specific needs when you RSVP.

Can I bring a plus one?

Due to venue capacity, we can only accommodate guests specifically named on your invitation. Thank you for understanding!

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive by 2:00 PM for the ceremony. Check our timeline above for the full schedule of events.

Will there be parking available?

Yes, there's plenty of free parking available at the venue. We'll also provide directions and transportation details closer to the date.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We'd love for you to enjoy the moment with us! Please feel free to take photos, but we ask that you keep flash photography to a minimum during the ceremony.
